    How to approach justice and ethics in your own work

    Published by Simon Du Plooy on the 27th of May 2025

    The main way to combat all of these potential problems is mindfulness. Mindfulness of your coding/process, of your final product and audience, of your potential collaborators/colleagues. In a group or work setting there is more structure and a push to embody these ideals. Justice and ethics in this context would be your behaviour and cohesion/synergy with the group. All of the perspectives and know-how of the team should be considered etc. Solo work and team work both also have justice and ethics in the context of the final product. The perception, accessibility, usability etc. of your product all factor into it's impact. When I work solo I would have to supplement the insight I would gain from a team via communication/research with others or findings to broaden my mindset.
