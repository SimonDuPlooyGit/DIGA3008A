    Blog post week 12

    Reflection on teachings

    Published by Simon Du Plooy on the 12th of May 2025

    I've considered my code's readability in the context of a team. How I structure functions, what comments I leave and how easy it is to understand my logic. This is a particular thing that has also crossed my mind mostly in GD class with my latest prototype. Where my character controller is quite messy in terms of logic. I did very little segmenting of code with almost no functions, most of the logic just occurred in the update function. Since we know "diverse teams build better products" I am reminded that your fellow collaborators are just as important to accommodate by using accessible design principles and conventions.

    In terms of Javascript I am still in the process of determing what best use I may have for it, and how I may change my website to suit the use of it, since in it's current state I feel my website needs a bit of a rework. Scaling is a current challenge and thinking of ways to change functionality or visuals on the site fundamentally between form factors seems to be a higher priority right now. This will also promote and create opportunity for me to use Javascript in a better way.
